About

About Me

I am a Computational Biologist and Machine Learning Scientist specializing in AI-driven biological discovery. My research integrates large language models, multimodal deep learning frameworks, and large-scale omics data to understand how proteins organize inside cells and how mutations drive disease.

Currently, I am developing network-based computational models integrating multi-omics data to identify disease mechanisms in immune disorders, applying machine learning and systems biology approaches to translate large-scale biological data into therapeutic insights.

I am passionate about building AI tools that make scientific discovery faster, more rigorous, and genuinely collaborative between humans and machines.
